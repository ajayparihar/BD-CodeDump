## Principles of Game Development

### Core Mechanics (5)
1. **Meaningful Choices** - Player decisions should have consequences
2. **Clear Goals** - Players must understand objectives
3. **Feedback Loops** - Immediate response to player actions
4. **Progression Systems** - Clear sense of advancement/achievement
5. **Risk vs Reward** - Balanced trade-offs in decisions

### Player Experience (5)
6. **Flow State** - Balance challenge with skill level
7. **Accessibility** - Playable by diverse abilities
8. **Tutorialization** - Teach through play, not just text
9. **Juiciness** - Excessive positive feedback for actions
10. **Fairness** - Players perceive system as just

### Technical (5)
11. **Deterministic Logic** - Same input = same output (replays, networking)
12. **Frame Independence** - Game speed not tied to frame rate
13. **State Synchronization** - Client/server states stay aligned
14. **Input Responsiveness** - Minimize latency between input and result
15. **Rollback/Interpolation** - Smooth networked experiences

### Economy & Balance (4)
16. **Resource Scarcity** - Limited resources drive decisions
17. **Counter Play** - No dominant strategy
18. **Catch-up Mechanics** - Losing players can recover
19. **Emergent Gameplay** - Simple rules create complex strategies

### Polish (4)
20. **Audio-Visual Feedback** - SFX/VFX confirm player actions
21. **UI Clarity** - HUD elements clear and glanceable
22. **Responsive Controls** - Inputs feel tight and immediate
23. **Consistent Visual Language** - UI patterns recognizable
24. **Performance Target** - Maintain stable frame rate
